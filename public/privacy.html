Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 6, 2025

1. Introduction

TradingPro ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

2.1 Information from OAuth Providers

  • Provider type (Google or Apple)
  • Provider user ID
  • Email address
  • Display name

2.2 Registration Information

  • Full name
  • Address
  • Phone number
  • Capital used for trading
  • Referral code (optional)

2.3 Usage Data

  • Login and logout timestamps
  • IP address
  • Browser type and user agent
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Trading activity and performance data

2.4 Payment Information

  • Payment proof screenshots
  • Transaction reference numbers
  • Payment dates and amounts

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Authenticate and verify your identity
  • Send important notifications about your account
  • Improve our platform and develop new features
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help us operate our platform (hosting, email, analytics)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS
  • Secure session management with httpOnly cookies
  • OAuth 2.0 / OpenID Connect for authentication (no password storage)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive information

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Login audit logs are retained for 2 years. Payment records are retained for 7 years as required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookie settings in your browser, but some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

9. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with third-party services (Google, Apple) for authentication. These services have their own privacy policies, which we recommend you review.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored and processed in India. If we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification.
