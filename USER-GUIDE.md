## üöÄ Getting Started

### What is TradingPro?

TradingPro is a subscription-based platform that provides access to premium trading tools, algorithms, educational content, and analytics. Subscribe to products monthly or yearly and get instant access once approved.

### System Requirements

- **Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
- **Internet**: Stable internet connection
- **Email**: Valid email address for notifications
- **Payment**: UPI payment app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)

## üí≥ Subscription Process

### Step 1: Add Product to Cart

1. Browse the product catalog
2. Select a product
3. Choose duration:
   - **Monthly**: Pay per month
   - **Yearly**: Pay for 12 months (discounted)
4. Select number of months (for monthly) or years (for yearly)
5. Click **"Add to Cart"**

**Example:**
- Product: Premium Trading Bot
- Base Price: ‚Çπ5,000/year or ‚Çπ500/month
- Selection: 3 months
- Total: ‚Çπ1,500

### Step 2: Review Your Cart

1. Click the **Cart icon** in navigation
2. Review your selections:
   - Product name
   - Duration selected
   - Price calculation
   - Subtotal

**Cart Actions:**
- ‚úèÔ∏è **Update Duration**: Change months/years
- üóëÔ∏è **Remove Item**: Delete from cart
- üõí **Continue Shopping**: Add more products
- ‚úÖ **Proceed to Checkout**: Go to payment

### Step 3: Checkout Process

1. Click **"Proceed to Checkout"**
2. Review order summary:
   - All items
   - Total amount
   - Duration breakdown

### Step 4: Make Payment

**Payment Method: UPI (QR Code)**

1. A QR code will be displayed
2. Open any UPI app:
   - Google Pay
   - PhonePe
   - Paytm
   - BHIM
   - Your bank's UPI app

3. Scan the QR code
4. Verify the amount
5. Complete the payment
6. Take a screenshot of the payment confirmation

**Payment Details Shown:**
- Merchant Name
- Amount to Pay
- UPI ID (for manual entry)

### Step 5: Upload Payment Proof

1. Click **"Upload Payment Proof"**
2. Select your screenshot:
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, PDF
   - Max size: 5 MB
3. Click **"Upload"**
4. Wait for confirmation

### Step 6: Order Confirmation

‚úÖ **Order Placed Successfully!**

You'll receive:
- On-screen confirmation
- Email to your registered email
- Order reference number

**What Happens Next:**
1. Admin reviews your payment proof
2. Order is approved (usually within 24 hours)
3. You receive approval email
4. Subscription becomes active
5. Access granted to product

## üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem**: Can't log in

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Check internet connection
- ‚úÖ Try different browser
- ‚úÖ Clear browser cache and cookies
- ‚úÖ Ensure popup blockers are disabled
- ‚úÖ Try incognito/private mode
- ‚úÖ Use correct OAuth provider (Google/Apple)

**Problem**: "Authentication failed"

**Solutions:**
- Grant all required permissions
- Check OAuth provider (Google/Apple) is accessible
- Try logging out and back in

### Registration Issues

**Problem**: Can't complete profile

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Fill ALL required fields
- ‚úÖ Use correct format:
  - Phone: 10 digits only (no spaces)
  - PAN: ABCDE1234F format
  - Aadhaar: 12 digits only
  - PIN: 6 digits
- ‚úÖ Ensure age is 18+
- ‚úÖ Check for typos

**Problem**: "Profile already completed"

**Solution:** You've already registered! Proceed to browse products.

### Cart Issues

**Problem**: Cart not updating

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Refresh the page
- ‚úÖ Check if product is still active
- ‚úÖ Try removing and re-adding item
- ‚úÖ Clear browser cache

**Problem**: Can't add to cart

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Ensure you're logged in
- ‚úÖ Complete profile first
- ‚úÖ Check if product is available

### Payment Issues

**Problem**: QR code not loading

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Refresh the page
- ‚úÖ Check internet connection
- ‚úÖ Try different browser
- ‚úÖ Contact support

**Problem**: Payment successful but can't upload proof

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Check file format (JPG/PNG/PDF only)
- ‚úÖ Ensure file size < 5 MB
- ‚úÖ Try compressing image
- ‚úÖ Try different file
- ‚úÖ Contact support with payment details

**Problem**: Upload failed

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Check file size (must be < 5 MB)
- ‚úÖ Check file format (JPG, PNG, or PDF)
- ‚úÖ Ensure image is clear and readable
- ‚úÖ Try uploading again
- ‚úÖ Contact support if persists

### Order Issues

**Problem**: Order stuck in "Pending"

**Solution:** 
- Wait 24 hours for admin review
- If > 24 hours, contact support with order ID
- Check spam folder for approval email

**Problem**: Order rejected

**Solution:**
- Check rejection reason in email
- Contact support with:
  - Order ID
  - Payment screenshot
  - Transaction ID
- Reorder if necessary

### Subscription Issues

**Problem**: Subscription not showing as active

**Solutions:**
- ‚úÖ Refresh dashboard

## ‚ùì FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

### General

**Q: What is TradingPro?**  
A: TradingPro is a subscription platform for trading tools, algorithms, educational content, and analytics.

**Q: Do I need to download anything?**  
A: No, TradingPro is entirely web-based. Access everything through your browser.

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and secure OAuth authentication.

### Account & Registration

**Q: Why do I need to register with personal details?**  
A: Per regulatory requirements, we need KYC information for financial services.

**Q: Can I use fake details?**  
A: No. Invalid details will result in account suspension.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**  
A: No. One account per user only.

**Q: Can I change my registered email?**  
A: Contact support for email changes.

**Q: I forgot my password**  
A: We use OAuth (Google/Apple), so you don't have a TradingPro password. Use your Google/Apple account.

### Products & Subscriptions

**Q: What products are available?**  
A: Browse the product catalog to see all offerings. Products include trading bots, analytics tools, educational content, etc.

**Q: Can I subscribe to multiple products?**  
A: Yes! Add as many products as you want to your cart.

**Q: Monthly vs Yearly - which is better?**  
A: Yearly subscriptions offer significant discounts (usually 15-20% off).

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**  
A: Contact support for cancellation requests. No automatic refunds for partial periods.

**Q: What happens when my subscription expires?**  
A: You lose access to the product. Resubscribe to regain access.

**Q: Do subscriptions auto-renew?**  
A: No. You must manually resubscribe before expiry.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Refund policy depends on circumstances. Contact support with your request.

### Payments

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: Currently, we accept UPI payments only.

**Q: Do you accept credit/debit cards?**  
A: Not currently. UPI only for now.

**Q: Is there a payment processing fee?**  
A: No additional fees. Pay the displayed amount only.

**Q: How long does approval take?**  
A: Usually within 24 hours, often much faster.

**Q: I made payment but forgot to upload proof. What do I do?**  
A: Contact support immediately with:
- Order ID
- Transaction ID
- Payment screenshot
- UPI reference number

**Q: Wrong amount paid. What should I do?**  
A: Contact support immediately. Do not place a new order.

**Q: Can I pay via bank transfer?**  
A: UPI only for now. Other methods may be added later.

### Technical Issues

**Q: Website is slow/not loading**  
A: Check your internet connection. Try clearing browser cache or using a different browser.

**Q: I see an error message**  
A: Take a screenshot and contact support with error details.

**Q: My browser isn't supported**  
A: We support all modern browsers. Update to the latest version.

### Support & Contact

**Q: How do I contact support?**  
A: Email: support@tradingpro.com (check footer for current email)

**Q: What information should I provide to support?**  
A:
- Your registered email
- Order ID (if applicable)
- Screenshot of issue
- Detailed description
- Browser/device information

**Q: How quickly will support respond?**  
A: Usually within 24 hours on business days.
